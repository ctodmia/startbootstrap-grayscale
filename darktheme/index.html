About Trenton

SMI is a social digital marketing agency giving small business owners strategies and tools to succeed in social media and online marketing. The challenge is integrating on-going messages created through text, images, and video and distributing it to the right audience at the right time.

Our mission is to help 10,000 small businesses benefit from our great service and low fees so they can generate more sales and grow. It’s not enough to perform a service. We want you to know the reasons for audience engagement leading to sales.

Leadership

As social media platforms grew in popularity, Trenton saw casual users become free to be themselves while watching small businesses stumble over the best ways to use sites like Facebook and Twitter. He saw social media as a subset of marketing and a critical link in the early phases of the sales process, yet few entrepreneurs were maximizing the potential. Trenton provided the leadership for building the staymobilestaysocial platform and the value-added services.
